# Supply Chain KPI Dictionary

| KPI | Definition | Business Use |
|---|---|---|
| OTIF | Orders delivered on time and in full / total orders | Service reliability |
| Fill Rate | Units shipped / units ordered | Demand fulfillment |
| Inventory Turnover | COGS / average inventory | Inventory efficiency |
| Days of Inventory | Average inventory / daily demand | Stock coverage |
| Forecast Accuracy | 1 - absolute forecast error / actual demand | Planning quality |
| Lead Time | Delivery date - order date | Responsiveness |
| Backorders | Orders not fulfilled by requested date | Service gap |
| Stockouts | SKU/location combinations with zero available inventory and demand | Availability risk |
| Cost-to-Serve | Freight + handling + exceptions cost by customer/order | Profitability control |
| On-Time Delivery | Shipments delivered by promised date / shipments | Carrier and fulfillment reliability |
| Warehouse Productivity | Units processed / labor hour | Operational efficiency |
