# Executive Summary Template

This portfolio lab produces a repeatable set of supply chain service, inventory, transportation and resilience metrics from synthetic ERP/WMS/TMS-style data.

## Questions for the operating review

1. Is OTIF deterioration caused by late delivery, partial fulfillment or both?
2. Which carrier combines strong service with acceptable freight cost per kilogram?
3. Which customer segments have high cost-to-serve relative to revenue?
4. Which SKU/warehouse combinations have fewer than five days of inventory?
5. Which suppliers contribute disproportionately to backorders and service failures?

## Decision framework

- **Protect service:** prioritize critical stockout and OTIF exceptions.
- **Reduce cost:** review carriers with high freight cost per kg and weak service.
- **Improve planning:** focus on categories with high weighted forecast error.
- **Strengthen resilience:** engage suppliers with high transparent risk scores.

The final version should replace this template with values from the generated outputs and clearly distinguish observations from recommendations.
